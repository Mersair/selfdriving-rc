Autonomous Driving

Manage your Car

To access your vehicle's dashboard:

  1. Power on the Raspberry Pi and Servo in your vehicle's AI/Sensors package
  2. Refresh this page. You should see your vehicle's name in the drop-down menu below
  3. Select your vehicle's name to be taken to your dashboard
  4. You will see your car's id, video feed, and the following options:
    • 'Start' - signals vehicle to start autonomously driving
    • 'Stop' - signals vehicle to stop driving
    • 'Top Speed' - change your vehicle's top speed from 0-100 percent of X throttle
    • 'Driving Mode'
      • Racing Mode - lowest latency driving by disabling sensors
      • Sensor Mode - higher latency driving with all sensors enabled
    • 'Driving Algorithm'
      • Low Latency Lane Following - most reliable
      • Raytracing
