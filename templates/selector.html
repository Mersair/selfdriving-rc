Autonomous Driving

Manage your Car

To access your vehicle's dashboard:

  1. Power on the Raspberry Pi and Servo in your vehicle's AI/Sensors package
  2. Refresh this page. You should see your vehicle's name in the drop-down menu below
  3. Select your vehicle's name to be taken to your dashboard. Once there, you will have three options:
    • 'Start' - signals vehicle to start autonomously driving
    • 'Stop' - signals vehicle to stop driving
    • 'Change Speed' - change your vehicle's top speed from 0.2-X throttle
